Geared by EIG

How to install: 
*Notes: Software cannot be run out of external device, user must have administrator access

If your organization has a webpage ask when where you can locate their download button. If not you can find a 
download button on their Facebook page where you can click it.

After downloading the zip folder extract it’s contents to a place of your choosing. Next open software folder from 
where you extracted it to. 

Next run BOTH Geared files as administrator. This can be achieved by right clicking them and selecting that option.

Congratulations! You have installed Geared, now that the hard work is done you can sit back and relax. On your 
computer's idle time you can watch as your spare resources are used to donate money to your organization.
